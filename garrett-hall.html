On-Campus Housing

Garrett Hall

Garrett Hall is a four-story, coed, building that accommodates approximately 73 residents. All living quarters consist of suites, and apartments, which contain a living room, three to four bedrooms and one to three baths. Suites are the same as apartments, but do not have kitchens. Most academic buildings and libraries are less than a 10-minute walk away and the South Campus Dining Hall is only five minutes away.
