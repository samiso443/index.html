On-Campus Housing

Cambridge Hall

Cambridge Hall is a four-story, coed, traditional style hall that accommodates close to 223 residents. Rooms range in size from single, double, triple and quad and each floor has a large community bathroom for residents to share. Located nearby are Byrd Stadium, the Campus Recreation Center, and the Stamp Student Union.
